Xtreme Keyboarding

RULES.

Compete with a friend, and be the faster typer by typing the next letter (highlighted in red)! But watch out! Try to be as accurate as possible so that you can trigger "bugs" that will slow your opponent down, making it harder for him/her to type. If you're affected by a bug, follow the specific instructions that show up at the bottom. May the faster typer win.
